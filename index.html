Fields of interest:

  • Macroeconomics;
  • Time series econometrics;
  • Economic geography;
  • Information economics.

My current research agenda focuses on the identification of hidden drivers behind macroeconomic phenomena and the estimation of their dynamic effects. In my work I use a variety of statistical and time series econometric tools -- presently my focus is on dimension reduction in dynamic settings, specifically multi-level (hierarchical) dynamic factor modeling. For these purposes I develop my own computational tools written using the Julia language, which I make available on GitHub (check out DynamicFactorModeling.jl).
